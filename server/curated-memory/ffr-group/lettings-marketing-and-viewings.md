# Lettings, Marketing and Viewings

## Core Lettings Workflow

- Inbound lead arrives from StuRents, website/Squarespace, WhatsApp, Facebook, accommodation groups, agents, society referrals or direct email.
- Triage same day: group size, course/student status, budget, move-in date, term length, property preference and urgency.
- Send Calendly booking link and keep viewing slots moving.
- Notify current tenants for access/viewings where required and preserve 24h notice discipline.
- View in person where possible. Bring contract or be ready to send immediately.
- Follow up within the first 3 hours after the viewing, ideally on WhatsApp if the prospect is already communicating there.
- Use urgency ethically: "we have other viewings" or "we can hold until Monday PM" only when true and commercially fair.
- Contract first, then deposit/standing order setup. International payments can need extra working days.

## Calendly and WhatsApp

- Calendly is a central viewing tool. It should handle reminders, thank-you flows and auto follow-ups.
- WhatsApp Business is high-conversion for student prospects and should be used for fast follow-up where compliant.
- Historical Sturents auto-reply pattern: thank lead, give Calendly link, say there are similar premium properties, invite WhatsApp conversation, sign as Hannah / PSB Properties.
- Do not hard-code historical phone numbers into agent prompts. Use the platform settings/current channel configuration.

## Pricing and Negotiation Pattern

- For 52OE, protect premium positioning and try for top prices when demand is strong.
- For FFR/HMO stock, the goal before Christmas is to get the student properties let and avoid dead stock.
- Use small incentives tactically rather than dropping rent too quickly: air fryer, wine/beer voucher, UberEats, HelloFresh, Netflix, Firestick or similar.
- When reducing, frame as time-limited if signing today and if Fergus/Hannah have approval.
- Contract signing in the office or face-to-face can lock momentum.
- Do not pause marketing until signatures/deposits are secure.

## Social and Flyer Messaging

- For Facebook groups, post as a person where group rules allow and ask prospects to comment or private message to arrange viewings.
- Use 3 strong teaser photos, postcode/location, "Central DH1 only" and "5 minutes from..." landmarks.
- Example positioning from chat: "4-7 bedroom properties available for 2026/27", central Durham including Elvet, Viaduct and Claypath, from GBP 173 pppw, bills included, premium standard, fully furnished and well maintained.
- Printed flyer/card idea: primary QR to Calendly booking link; secondary QR to photos/site. Main CTA should be "Scan Here To Book Viewing."

## Arrears and Collections

- Standing orders should be set up for the 1st of each month.
- Late payers should be chased quickly. For serious arrears, reference contractual late payment interest only after checking the actual contract and legal position.
- Where joint and several liability applies, the lead tenant/flatmates can be copied carefully, but privacy and legal tone matter.
- For Renters Rights Act/Renters Rights Bill context, confirm exact commencement dates and transition rules from current law before sending any external notice.
