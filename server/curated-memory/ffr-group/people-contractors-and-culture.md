## Contractor Control Rules

- Track contractor days, arrival times, departure times, scope, photos, materials, invoices and source WhatsApp/email evidence.
- Florin/Romanian team cost benchmark from chat: about GBP 20 per hour per worker, roughly GBP 250 per worker per full day, four workers about GBP 1,000/day. This is valuable for high-output jobs but expensive for small low-value tasks.
- Use high-cost multi-trade teams for high-value jobs: bathrooms, windows, HMO/fire remediation, doors/closers, sash windows, heavy refurb. Avoid burning that day rate on low-value tasks when cheaper local options exist.
- If an invoice hours total does not match internal worksheets, ask for a detailed breakdown and treat confirmed lower hour notes as the basis for reconciliation.
- Keep photographic evidence and daily notes because memory from WhatsApp alone is not reliable enough for invoice disputes.

## Weekly Reporting Culture

Fergus asked for an end-of-week email covering:

- How many contracts are signed.
- Amount and property/unit.
- Major maintenance issues or meaningful outstanding agenda items.
- Spend for the week.

Major issues should be highlighted clearly. Minor tenant noise such as small cosmetic requests should not swamp the report.

## Operating Culture

- The business is intentionally not a normal company. Flexibility is paired with high expectations, fast learning and continuous improvement.
- Fergus repeatedly frames complacency as one of the biggest business risks.
- AI should be used as a serious operator tool, not a toy: read whole chains, retrieve old deals, draft in the right tone, surface missing source evidence and save hours.
- WhatsApp is useful for speed but creates memory loss. FFR Property OS should convert WhatsApp/email/calendar/document context into tasks, ledgers, approvals and source files.
- Tone for tenants/prospects should usually be warm, clear and commercially firm. For legal/rent arrears matters, drafts should be checked for compliance and written like a careful English landlord/housing lawyer.
