- `FFR Group Limited` is the family property company for the wider Durham and St Andrews portfolio. It is described in the operating manual as owned by Peter, Philippa, Fergus, Ruari and Flora, with Peter, Philippa and Fergus as directors. Banking context includes Starling. Operating approval thresholds from the Hannah manual:

- Hannah can approve works up to GBP 500.
- GBP 500 to GBP 1,000 requires message approval from Fergus.
- Over GBP 1,000 requires written approval and, where structural or significant, two quotes.
- Agents must require approval for legal positions, external send actions, rent/deposit concessions, access notices, contractor instructions, supplier commitments, payments and any irreversible calendar/listing change.

## Financial Entity Logic

- FFR Starling, PSB52 Wise and PSB52 Tide need consolidated reporting with intercompany/internal transfers eliminated.
- PSB52 Wise to Tide sweeps are internal and should be eliminated in group views.
- Tide lease payments to P P Bell Partnership are included in provided-account P&L, then added back in the group bridge where a 52 Old Elvet PropCo/NatWest layer is estimated separately.
- Pleo is a maintenance/team spend wallet and should not stay as an unexplained suspense line. Pull the Pleo ledger where possible.
- Paragon and Landbay mortgage payments in FFR bank models are often gross because the statement does not split principal and interest.
